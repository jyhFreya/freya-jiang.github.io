Research Interests

Embodied AI Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Reinforcement Learning Humanoid Robotics Robot Learning

My research focuses on developing intelligent embodied agents that can perceive, reason, and interact with the physical world. I am particularly interested in combining vision-language models with reinforcement learning to enable robots to understand natural language instructions and execute complex manipulation tasks in unstructured environments.

Research Projects

Embodied Humanoid Robot

Ongoing

Working on developing humanoid robots capable of performing complex manipulation and locomotion tasks in human-centric environments. The research integrates Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models with reinforcement learning to enable the robot to understand multimodal instructions, perceive its surroundings, and execute dexterous manipulation tasks. Focus areas include whole-body control, sim-to-real transfer, and learning from human demonstrations.

VLA Models Reinforcement Learning Sim-to-Real Transfer Whole-body Control Imitation Learning

Medical Robotics System

Completed

Contributed to the development of robotic systems designed for medical applications, focusing on enhancing precision and safety in clinical procedures. The project involved designing robot control algorithms, sensor integration, and human-robot interaction interfaces to assist healthcare professionals in surgical or diagnostic tasks. Emphasized reliability, accuracy, and compliance with medical safety standards.

Robot Control Sensor Fusion Human-Robot Interaction Medical Device Standards

Textile Defect Detection System

Completed

Developed an AI-powered visual inspection system for automated textile defect detection in industrial manufacturing environments. The system leverages deep learning-based computer vision techniques, including convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and attention mechanisms, to identify and classify various types of fabric defects such as holes, stains, weaving errors, and color inconsistencies. Achieved high detection accuracy while maintaining real-time processing speeds suitable for production line deployment.

Computer Vision Deep Learning Image Segmentation Real-time Processing
